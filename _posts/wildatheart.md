Wild At Heart is a new EP by Lunettes. This 5-song extended play also marks the arrival of Serina Farina (of The Moon Snails) as a new member of Lunettes. 

Tyler and Serina were inspired by David Lynch's film "Wild At Heart" and aptly wrote a song (and EP) of the same name.
